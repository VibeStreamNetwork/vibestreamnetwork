Who We Are

VIBE STREAM NETWORK



Vibe Stream Network is a major disruptor of the entertainment industry. We reinvent outdated processes that have historically been a barrier to growth for artists and fans. VSN sees a more equitable entertainment industry burgeoning from blockchain technology: a world where creators “Treat their Art like Assets”, where fans can be investors rather than consumers, where brands and businesses streamline licensing deals… a world where content is currency.
